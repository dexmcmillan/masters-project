IV. Reporter's Notes

A happy coincidence of doing this reporting is that I was able to do so without taking a plane at all. Planes are responsible for a massive amount of GHG emissions, so I travelled by car and train to get where I needed to go.

Driving across Canada is a fantastic experience, but das I hit Manitoba, I hit the nastiest rain storm I had ever seen.
